About Me

My name is Sugandh Khanna, and I have 10 years of experience in the IT industry. I began my career with a startup, where I focused on Drupal technology back in 2013. During my time there, I successfully created a website using Drupal CMS, laying the foundation for my expertise in this field. Over the years, I expanded my skill set to include various technologies such as Node.js, PHP, and AWS, working on more than 60 websites across different industries.

I am proud to hold certifications in Drupal, AWS, and Agile Scrum Master, which have further validated my knowledge and capabilities. In my previous organization, I spent over three years working on an ecommerce project built on a headless architecture. Initially, I was responsible for developing REST APIs from scratch and seamlessly integrating them with the React frontend team to deliver dynamic content. This role introduced me to Akamai CDN and AWS, where I gained hands-on experience in leveraging caching mechanisms and optimizing content delivery.

As my career progressed, I transitioned to a leadership position, overseeing a team of 30 members. I ensured that APIs were efficiently processed through Akamai cache and pregen layers, maximizing performance and reducing load on the stateless servers. Additionally, I successfully managed 64 brand websites hosted on a distributed network, consistently ensuring their availability and performance.

Currently, I am working as a Project Manager, juggling four projects simultaneously. One of these projects revolves around Drupal, where I focus on optimizing site performance by leveraging content delivery networks and efficient asset management. Another project involves integrating APIs with CRM HubSpot, enhancing data management and customer interactions. Additionally, I am actively involved in the development of a healthcare project in Germany, leading a team of five developers. Lastly, I am in the requirements analysis phase of an ongoing project.

Throughout my career, I have consistently demonstrated my commitment to optimizing website performance, managing critical incidents such as DDoS attacks, and leveraging cutting-edge technologies like Akamai CDN and AWS. I thrive in dynamic environments and enjoy leading teams towards successful project outcomes.
